About Thomas Webber

Professional Biography

Born in 1982 in Schützenbrunnen, Switzerland, Thomas Webber grew up in a family deeply rooted in banking. From his father and grandfather, he inherited both knowledge and values, developing an early passion for finance, investments, and understanding how financial systems shape people's lives.

After completing his education, Thomas began his career at Credit Suisse and later ING Group, gaining over seven years of experience in wealth management, corporate finance, and international investments.

Yet, he became increasingly disillusioned with traditional banking, where client interests were too often overshadowed by institutional profit. Choosing integrity over corporate comfort, Thomas transitioned into independent financial advisory, committed to delivering transparent, client-first solutions.

He strengthened his expertise with roles at UBS Wealth Management and Allianz Global Investors, gaining deeper knowledge in portfolio strategy, retirement planning, and alternative investments.

Since 2019, Thomas has been a senior advisor at H&W Invest, where he empowers individuals and families to secure their financial futures through transparent, ethical, and client-centered advisory services.

Thomas is recognized across Europe for his independence, professionalism, and an unwavering commitment to protecting and growing his clients' wealth.
